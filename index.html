Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI flashcard maker work?

Enter a topic, a question, or upload a PDF. The AI reads your input, detects the right exam context, and generates a set of flip flashcards with a clear question on the front and a concise answer on the back.

What are Preset Decks?

Preset decks are pre-made flashcard sets for popular exam topics — no AI generation needed and no token used. Click any topic card in the Preset Decks tab to open it immediately.

Which exams are supported?

Class 12 CBSE, CUET, NEET, JEE, Banking, SSC, NTA / UGC NET, and a general "Other" mode for any other subject or certification.

Is there a daily limit?

Yes — 5 AI generations per day and 20 per week. Preset decks are unlimited. If an AI generation fails for any reason, your token is automatically refunded.

Can I download my flashcards?

Yes. After generating a deck, click "Download" to save a standalone HTML file that works completely offline with ThunderStudy branding and links included.

Can I save decks for later?

Yes. Click "Save" after generating to store up to 5 decks in your browser. Saved decks can be reloaded any time without using a token.
